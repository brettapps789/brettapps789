This chapter serves as your gateway into understanding how AI and smart home ecosystems are reshaping daily experiences, especially for individuals with disabilities, caregivers, and progressive small business owners seeking to integrate cutting-edge, inclusive technology solutions. ## Understanding AI and Smart Home Ecosystems Artificial Intelligence is a broad term used to describe computer systems that mimic human intelligence to perform tasks. From learning and problem-solving to recognizing speech and making decisions, AI’s capabilities are vast and continually expanding. When incorporated into home automation, AI acts as the brain of the smart home, orchestrating various devices and systems to function seamlessly together. Smart home technology refers to homes equipped with devices that can be controlled remotely through a smartphone app or a centralized system. These devices include smart speakers, lighting systems, thermostats, and security systems, all capable of communicating with each other to create a harmonious and responsive living environment.
